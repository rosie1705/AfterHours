About Us

We are a passionate team of digital media students bringing creativity, energy, and collaboration to life through music and technology. Our event is a celebration of student talent — from electrifying DJ sets to immersive digital visuals — all crafted to mark the end of the semester in style.

This one-night-only festival blends electronic music, interactive media, and student culture into an unforgettable experience. Powered by creativity and community, we aim to provide a vibrant platform for emerging DJs, visual artists, and media creatives to shine.

Whether you're here to dance, connect, or simply enjoy the vibe — you're part of something special.
